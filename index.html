About Manai Restaurant

Established in 2005, Manai Restaurant has been serving authentic Nigerian cuisine to the people of Demba and beyond.

Our chefs use traditional recipes passed down through generations, combined with the freshest local ingredients to create dishes that delight the senses.

We take pride in our commitment to quality, flavor, and excellent customer service.
